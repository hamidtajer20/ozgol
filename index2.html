Who is the developer?

It should be noted that the developer's field of work is not limited to mobile or web application design and their development, and some of them work on technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, etc., which are much more difficult and advanced.

Front-end developer

In general, a front-end developer is a developer who designs and develops the front end of a site. In other words, everything you see when you enter a site, such as colors, icons, etc., is the work of a front-end developer. To become a front-end developer, you must know languages ​​such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and gain sufficient mastery of them. It should be noted that you can refer to educational resources on YouTube or the web3school website to learn these languages.
